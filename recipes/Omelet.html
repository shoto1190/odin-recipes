Cally's Omelet

Description

"The best omelet in the world!" -Still up for debate...

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ red bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ Bermuda onion, sliced
  • 7 baby portobello mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ pound beef tip
  • ½ cup egg substitute

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in green bell pepper, red bell pepper, onion and portobello mushrooms.
  3. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir beef into the vegetable mixture, and cook until evenly brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Stir egg beaters into the mixture, and cook 10 minutes, or until firm.
